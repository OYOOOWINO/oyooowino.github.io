LECOMMET STONE CUTTING MACHINE

Description
The fundamental operation consist in cutting the quarry stone vertically in one direction,and hortogonally in a second one .Use also a horizontal disk that causes the block detachment. The company is available for technical assistance in the quarry via Daniel and Joseph in Kenya and other regions in Africa.
Construction
All the materials employed in the construction of our machines are of highest quality. All the revolving parts move in ball bearings and cogwheels are enclosed in oil-sealed casings .The machine has been projected to guarantee over-high working performance.The machine controls are easy and intuitive .The heavy weight provides stability. Universal and heavy machine ideal for big productions but with high flexibility of use.
24 Hours Production Capacity

20,000 -30,000 to 40,000 BLOCKS (according to the blocks size)

Operational Specifications
  • FORWARD CUTTING SPEED: 0-20 minute meters
  • QUICK RETURN STAGE: 0-120 minute meters
  • RAIL TRACK GUAGE: 730 mm
  • CUTTING DEPTH FOR VERTICAL BLADES: aprox 0-400 mm
  • HORIZONTAL BLADES: 0-300 mm
  • MAXIMUM DEPTH FOR HORIZONTAL BLADES: positioning 410 mm
  • ADAPTABILITY OF THE CUTTING AND FORWARD SPEED ACCORDING TO QUARRY STONE
